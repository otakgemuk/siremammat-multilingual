Raschke 12 Rules

Linda Raschke's 12 Rules of Trading coded into Pine Script with a real-time health tracking dashboard.

Overview

The SireMammat Edition includes all 12 rules from Linda Raschke's methodology with implementation status and a real-time table tracking technical health across three key dimensions.

Linda Raschke is a veteran trader with 35+ years of experience. Her 12 rules represent the distilled wisdom of decades of professional trading.

This indicator monitors compliance with all 12 rules in real-time and displays a dashboard showing which rules are currently satisfied and which are being violated.

How It Works

The 12 Rules

  • Rule 1: Trade with the trend — always
  • Rule 2: Use stops — always. No exceptions.
  • Rule 3: Keep losses small. Let winners run.
  • Rule 4: Never add to a losing position
  • Rule 5: Don't trade too many markets at once
  • Rule 6: Don't trade in unfamiliar markets
  • Rule 7: Always use a trading plan
  • Rule 8: Never trade without a stop loss
  • Rule 9: Cut losses, let profits run
  • Rule 10: Don't follow the crowd
  • Rule 11: Learn from your mistakes
  • Rule 12: Keep a trading journal

Real-Time Health Dashboard

  • Three-panel display: Trend Health, Risk Health, Execution Health
  • Green = rule satisfied, Red = rule being violated
  • Alerts when a rule violation is detected
  • Helps maintain discipline during live trading
