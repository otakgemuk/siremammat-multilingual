M2B/M2S Setup

Catches two-legged pullbacks to the 20 EMA with price-action confirmation. Disciplined pullback entries, not chasing breakouts.

Two-Legged Pullback20 EMA TouchM2B Long / M2S ShortMulti-Timeframe5-min to 1H

Overview

M2B/M2S is a mean-reversion entry system that waits for the market to pull back to a 20 EMA in two consecutive legs, then confirms a rejection with a candle close back on the trend side.

The indicator is designed for index futures traders who want disciplined pullback entries rather than chasing breakouts. It works on any timeframe but is most reliable on 5-minute through 1-hour charts.

Built on Al Brooks' methodology: the two-legged pullback to the EMA is one of the highest probability setups in a trending market.

How It Works

M2B (Long) — Uptrend Pullback Entry

  • Requires strong uptrend: series of HH/HL, price above 20 EMA
  • Waits for two-legged pullback down to the 20 EMA
  • Confirms rejection: candle closes back above EMA with lower wick
  • Entry: buy stop 1 tick above signal bar high
  • Stop: 1 tick below the 2nd leg pullback low

M2S (Short) — Downtrend Pullback Entry

  • Requires strong downtrend: series of LL/LH, price below 20 EMA
  • Waits for two-legged rally up to the 20 EMA
  • Confirms rejection: candle closes back below EMA with upper wick
  • Entry: sell stop 1 tick below signal bar low
  • Stop: 1 tick above the 2nd leg rally high

Key Rules

  • Do NOT enter on the first leg — always wait for the second test
  • Cancel order if not triggered within 2 bars — setup is stale
  • Never widen the stop — if thesis is wrong, take the loss
  • Move stop to breakeven once 50% to first target reached

Signal Types

M2B Long

Two-legged pullback to 20 EMA in uptrend. Buy above signal bar.

Conviction: High
M2S Short

Two-legged rally to 20 EMA in downtrend. Sell below signal bar.

Conviction: High

Best Conditions

Strong Trend

EMA must be rising (long) or falling (short). Flat EMA = skip.

Clean Two-Leg Pullback

A-B-C structure must be clear. Messy pullbacks = skip.

EMA Touch

2nd leg must touch or come within 1-2 ticks of EMA.

No News

Skip if FOMC, CPI, NFP within 30 minutes.
