Overview

Sire-GreenLight Core is a trend-continuation momentum indicator built for index futures traders. It identifies high-probability entries by tracking consecutive directional bars against a 20 EMA bias and a 4-layer filter system.

The indicator produces three signal types — L8/B8 PRO (highest conviction), L4/B4 (early warning), and Gap 20 EMA (continuation alert) — each representing a different level of momentum confirmation.

Designed for ES, NQ, MES, and MNQ on 5-minute through 1-hour charts. Works best during RTH (Regular Trading Hours) when volume and liquidity are highest.

How It Works

The 4-Filter System

  • Filter 1: 20 EMA Direction — price must be trending relative to the 20 EMA
  • Filter 2: Consecutive Bars — counts directional bars for momentum confirmation
  • Filter 3: Volume Confirmation — volume must support the momentum move
  • Filter 4: Price Position — where price closes within the bar matters

Signal Generation

  • L8/B8 PRO: 8 consecutive bars in trend direction — highest conviction entry
  • L4/B4: 4 consecutive bars — early warning, prepare for entry
  • Gap 20 EMA: Price gaps away from EMA — continuation alert
  • All signals include stop-loss levels and take-profit targets
