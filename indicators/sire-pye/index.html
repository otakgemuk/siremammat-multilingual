Overview

Sire-PYE is a trend-following scalp system designed for Index Futures (MNQ / MES / NQ / ES). It uses a higher-timeframe 'Anchor' EMA to determine market direction and executes on a lower timeframe to enter pullbacks.

The core philosophy is aggressive risk management: it prioritizes capital preservation by utilizing a strict daily Kill Switch that prevents overtrading during choppy, non-trending sessions.

Backtested +34.19% on MNQ over 12 months with a 1.83 profit factor. Designed for traders who want consistent small gains with strict downside protection.

How It Works

The Anchor EMA System

  • Uses a 30-min 20 EMA as the 'anchor' — determines overall trend direction
  • Price above anchor = bullish bias, below = bearish bias
  • Only trades in the direction of the anchor — no counter-trend entries
  • Lower timeframe (5-min) entries on pullbacks to the anchor level

Chop Ratio Filter

  • Measures market choppiness in real-time
  • High chop ratio = market is ranging — reduce size or skip
  • Low chop ratio = market is trending — full position size
  • Prevents entries during the 70% of time markets spend in ranges

Daily Kill Switch

  • Triggers when daily loss exceeds predefined threshold
  • Once triggered — no more trades for the day. Period.
  • Prevents revenge trading and emotional decision-making
  • Resets at start of each new trading day

Signal Types

Long Entry

Price pulls back to 30-min EMA in uptrend. Chop filter confirms trending.

Conviction: High
Short Entry

Price rallies to 30-min EMA in downtrend. Chop filter confirms trending.

Conviction: High
Kill Switch

Daily loss threshold hit. No more trades. Capital preserved.

Conviction: Protective
