Sire-SchoolFishing

Combines Tom Hougaard's School Run (30-minute opening range bracketing) with the Fishing Technique and a 9/20 EMA foundation.

Overview

Sire-SchoolFishing is a multi-strategy indicator built around three proven trading methodologies from Tom Hougaard's price-action playbook. Each module operates independently but they're designed to complement each other.

The indicator is designed for index futures traders who trade the NY cash session (09:30 – 16:00 ET). It works best on 5-minute or 15-minute charts.

Three modules: School Run (opening range breakout), Fishing Technique (stop-run mean reversion), and EMA Foundation (trend bias). Use them together for confluence or independently for specific setups.

How It Works

Module 1: School Run (30-min Opening Range)

  • Brackets the high and low of the first 30 minutes of RTH
  • Classifies the day: Trend (breakout holds), Range (oscillates), Failed (breakout reverses)
  • Each classification has its own entry and exit playbook
  • Use 5-min bars from 09:30 ET to construct the bracket

Module 2: Fishing Technique (Stop-Run)

  • Catches liquidity grabs when price dips below swing lows or spikes above swing highs
  • Buy signal: price dips below swing low, candle closes back above — stop-run confirmed
  • Sell signal: price spikes above swing high, candle closes back below — stop-run confirmed
  • Mean-reversion model — works best in range/balanced markets

Module 3: 9/20 EMA Foundation

  • 9 EMA above 20 EMA = bullish bias — look for longs only
  • 9 EMA below 20 EMA = bearish bias — look for shorts only
  • EMAs flat/tangled = no trend — reduce size or sit out
  • Always check EMA bias before any School Run or Fishing trade
